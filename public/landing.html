Features

Everything You Need

From product 360s to cinematic launch reels, create any video with code.

3D Model Rendering

Cinematic reveals, product 360° turns, and hero shots from .glb/.gltf models with studio or dramatic lighting.

HTML Scene Authoring

Motion graphics, kinetic typography, explainers, and multi-scene launch reels with HTML/GSAP/Three.js.

Video Editor Preview

Scrub frame-by-frame in the browser, then render. No blind renders — what you preview is exactly what you get.

Transparent Output

Export ProRes 4444 .mov with alpha channel for compositing in Premiere, Final Cut, or DaVinci.

Reference Matching

Analyze a reference video at 30fps and use its pacing, motion, palette, and composition as generation cues.

Deterministic

CI-tested golden frames ensure no drift between renders. Re-run produces pixel-identical output.
